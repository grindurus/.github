# GrindURUS

Automated Market Taker

GrindURUS is a DeFi infrastructure designed to extract value from market price volatility through automated execution.

---

## What is GrindURUS?

GrindURUS is not a passive investment strategy.

It is an execution system that continuously interacts with the market to generate value from price movements.

Instead of relying on long-term price growth, GrindURUS operates on:

- volatility
- price oscillations
- continuous trading activity

---

## Strategy

GrindURUS uses an Automated Market Taker (AMT) model.

The system executes two complementary strategies:

- **DIRECT** — buy low → sell high  
- **INVERSE** — sell high → buy low  

By operating on both sides of the market, GrindURUS:

- captures spreads from price movement  
- maintains dynamic inventory exposure  
- generates continuous execution flow  

---

## Why it works

Traditional strategies depend on direction:

- price goes up → profit  
- price goes down → loss  

GrindURUS depends on movement itself.

Example: 100 → 110 → 100

- HODL → no profit  
- GrindURUS → captures volatility

## GRAI

GRAI is an index token representing a share of the system.

It aggregates:

- idle capital  
- active capital deployed in strategies  
- accumulated yield  

GRAI allows users to:

- gain exposure to the system  
- participate without managing execution  
- redeem value proportionally

## Architecture

GrindURUS consists of:
